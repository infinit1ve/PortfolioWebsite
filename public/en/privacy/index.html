Privacy Policy

Last updated: 28.06.2025

This Privacy Policy explains how your data is handled when you visit infinitive.cc. This website is operated by a private individual based in Bavaria, Germany.

1. No Personal Data Collected

This website does not collect, store, or process any personal data. It does not use:

  • Cookies
  • Analytics tools
  • Tracking scripts
  • User accounts or logins

Your visit is fully anonymous from the website owner's perspective.

2. Server and Infrastructure Logging

While this site itself does not track users, it is hosted and served via the following services:

  • Cloudflare (DNS, CDN, DDoS protection)
  • Render (web hosting)

These providers may log standard technical data for security and operational reasons, including:

  • IP Address
  • Date and time of access
  • Browser type and version
  • Referrer URL
  • Device/OS information

These logs are not accessible to the site owner and are handled according to the privacy policies of the respective providers:

  • Cloudflare Privacy Policy
  • Render Privacy Policy

3. Email and Contact

If you send an email to contact@infinitive.cc, your email address and content will be stored solely for the purpose of replying. Your data will not be shared, sold, or used for any other purpose.

Emails are processed via Zoho Mail, which complies with GDPR and offers secure data handling.

4. Embedded Content

Some pages may include content from other websites (e.g. embedded images, videos, fonts, or links). Embedded content from other websites behaves as if you visited those sites directly. These sites may collect data, use cookies, or track your interaction — according to their own privacy policies.

5. Legal Basis

The legal bases for processing, as defined in Art. 6 GDPR, are:

  • Art. 6(1)(f) – Legitimate interest (server logs for security, spam protection, etc.)
  • Art. 6(1)(b) – Contract fulfillment (replying to your email if you contact me)

6. Your Rights

Under the GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data held about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict processing
  • Lodge a complaint with a data protection authority

Since this website stores no personal data about visitors, most of these rights are not applicable here — but feel free to reach out at contact@infinitive.cc with any questions.

7. Data Storage Location

Any data processed by infrastructure providers (Cloudflare and Render) may be stored on servers in the EU, USA, or other regions, depending on their infrastructure setup. These providers implement appropriate safeguards for international data transfers.

8. External Links

This website may contain links to external websites that are not operated by me. Please be aware that I have no control over the content or privacy practices of these websites. I encourage you to review the privacy policies of any external sites you visit.

I am not responsible for the protection and privacy of any information you provide while visiting such sites.

9. Contact

If you have any privacy-related concerns, you can contact me at contact@infinitive.cc

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in legal, technical, or operational requirements. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
