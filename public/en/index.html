A bit about me

Currently I am studying at a German FOS and enjoy doing things — whether that's coding, tinkering with Linux, playing games, or writing, I'm up for it. I'm always looking to improve through learning so I could reach my personal goals. And this webpage is an extension of that — my personal portfolio and a small portal where I share my thoughts with the world.
The decision to make this page a reality came spontaneously, much like many of my ideas. Realistically, I'm not chasing perfection — I'm chasing expirience, though I wouldn't mind doing everything flawlessly either.
